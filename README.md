# Dream Weaver

A private, on-device companion for lucid dreamers, astral projectors, and reality shifters.

Dream Weaver is a mobile app that helps you record what happens at night, track your practice, and see your patterns over time — without sending a single byte to a server. Everything you write stays on your phone.

---

## What it does

Dream Weaver gives you four connected spaces:

- **Dream Journal** — Log dreams the moment you wake. Tag entries (people, places, themes, emotions), mark them as lucid, astral or shifts, and export polished PDFs. Use the *Journal* type for freeform notes — meditation reflections, daily thoughts, anything that isn't strictly a dream.
- **Attempt Tracker** — Log each practice session: the technique you used, the sensations you noticed, conditions like sleep quality, and the outcome. Spot what actually works for you.
- **DR Script Builder** — Design your Desired Reality with sections for identity, world, people, story and scenes. Already write scripts elsewhere? Import a fillable template instead. Export as PDF and link scripts to shifting attempts.
- **Experience Wishlist** — A bucket list of experiences you want to have in a dream or astral projection. Tick them off and share your progress as a card or PDF.

---

## How you use it

1. **Start logging.** Open the Journal tab, hit the plus button, and write what you remember. Add tags as you go — they're how everything connects.
2. **Search and reflect.** Filter by tag, title, or type. Hit the bar-chart pill in the search row to see a snapshot of your totals across 7 days, 30 days, or all time — scoped to whatever filter you have active.
3. **Track your practice.** Use the Tracker tab to log attempts and review what's been working.
4. **Build and link scripts.** Open the Wishlist, then Scripts. Build out your DR, then link it to a shifting attempt when you try.
5. **Share or export anything.** Every screen has a Share or PDF option. Use Privacy Mode (eye icon or shake your phone) to hand the device to someone without exposing your entries.
6. **Back up whenever.** The Progress tab can export a full backup of your dreams, attempts, scripts and wishlist. Import the file later to restore everything.

---

## Privacy

Dream Weaver is local-only by design.

- No accounts, no sign-ups
- No cloud sync, no servers
- No analytics, no tracking
- All entries live in secure on-device storage

Your dream life stays yours.
